Quiz Answer Key and Fun Facts

1. When Mr. Furley was out of town, who was feeding his fish?

Answer: Cindy

When Jack, Janet and Cindy were talking in the apartment, Cindy excused herself so she could go feed Furley's goldfish. Furley had gone away on a single's cruise.
2. Helen told Jack and Janet that it was her 25th wedding anniversary.

Answer: True

She was upset that Stanley did not take her out for a romantic dinner among other things, so she left him. She needed to go somewhere and did not know where to go so she went to the trio's apartment knowing that would help her out.
3. What did Stanley get tickets to on their anniversary?

Answer: A bowling banquet

Helen told Jack and Janet that Stanley was given two tickets to a bowling banquet and he was going to take a friend with him.

4. Whose idea was it for Helen to spend the night in Mr. Furley's apartment?

Answer: Cindy

Cindy came up with the idea since Furley would not be home for another day or more. Janet said that Helen could sleep in Jack's room and Jack sleep on the couch. Helen said that she could stay at a hotel in a seedy neighborhood.
5. Who came up with the idea that Helen should try to make Stanley jealous?

Answer: Janet

It was Janet who came up with the idea. Larry was not in this episode. Later in the episode Jack basically gave her the "I told you so" speech.
6. Stanley went to the trio's apartment. What did he want to drink before he went to talk to Helen?

Answer: Brandy

Stanley's favorite drink when they were on the show was cocoa. He rarely drank alcohol on the show but when he did it was usually brandy.

7. Who took Stanley down to Furley's apartment?

Answer: Jack

Jack took Stanley to Furley's apartment. He left once Stanley was in the living room. Jack was not aware that Furley had returned and was in bed with Helen.
8. When Stanley told Helen that he had given her the best years of his life, she said that it was 1955 and part of 1956.

Answer: False

Helen said it was 1956 and part of 1957. This was the last episode that would show Helen and Stanley.
9. When Jack went to get Stanley from the Regal Beagle, who was the bartender?

Answer: Jim

Mike became the bartender later in the episode. George and Fred never tended bar at the Regal Beagle.

A3: A caftan.
Q4: What is the name of Mrs. Roper's often-irritated husband?
A4: Stanley (played by Norman Fell).
Q5: What was the street address of the Santa Monica apartment building the Ropers managed?
A5: 201 West Hampton Drive.
