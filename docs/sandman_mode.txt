🏖️ Sandman
Mode idea: “Shifting Sands” 
- moving drops
- raise right drops, all 5 LEDs light up, the leftmost LED flashing
- points: flashing one worth 50K, others 1K.
- drop one at a time every 3 seconds (L to R)
  - if next drop already down, move to next one 
- every 5 completed while they are flashing will be a 250K JP
- if 5 completed L to R - 500K JP
- once all are down, reset bank 
- repeat 3 times
