# 🧪 GOBLIN CHAOS MULTIBALL

### Start:

* 2-ball multiball begins
* Chaos Bonus = 0
* Chaos Lock = 0

### During Chaos:

* 3 random shots flashing
* after 5 sec flashing → solid
* after 3 sec solid → off/new random flashing set

### Shot scoring:

#### flashing hit:

* 50K live score
* +50K Chaos Bonus

#### solid hit:

* penalty shot
* only 2K live score
* deduct 50K from Chaos Bonus
* but never below Chaos Lock

---

# 🧠 SAUCER = CHAOS STABILIZER

When either ball enters saucer:

* current Chaos Bonus becomes `Chaos Lock`
* held ball stays for 10 sec
* all currently active scoring shots flash safely
* no penalty shots during hold window
* player gets controlled single-ball harvest time

Then:

* saucer eject
* random chaos pattern resumes

Each time saucer lock occurs:

* next hold time decreases by 1 second down to min 5 seconds
* flashing duration shrinks from 5 sec to 4 sec to 3 sec

> Goblin momentarily cornered, then escapes.

Players understand:
“I secured my gains, now I can push higher.”

---

### Chaos Bonus:

* continuously vulnerable above Chaos Lock
* paid at end of ball

---




# SCORING ARC EXAMPLE

* build Chaos to 300K
* saucer locks 300K
* controlled 10 sec builds to 550K
* eject chaos resumes
* solid hits knock back to 450K but not below 300K
* build to 700K
* drain one ball, continue mode/end ball eventually collect

This is compelling.
