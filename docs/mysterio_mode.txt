🎭 Mysterio
Mode idea: “Illusion Trial”

- like find Barb - stranger things
- 10 shots lit, one has the super jackpot 
- super worth 1M , each incorrect shot worth 50K (deducts 100K from super)



Because Mysterio is a mode where the *callouts sell the illusion mechanic.*

Using Spider-Man intuition instead of generic “left/right hint” makes it feel integrated into theme instead of abstract puzzle logic.

---

# 🎭 MYSTERIO — “ILLUSION TRIAL” FINAL SHAPE

## Core fiction:

Mysterio clouds reality.

Spider-Man uses **Spidey Sense** to narrow where the real threat is.

That is a perfect narrative wrapper.

---

# 🔊 CLUE CALLOUT STYLE

Instead of sterile clues:

* “Spidey Sense tells me it’s to the right…”
* “Something feels wrong on the left…”
* “The danger isn’t upstairs…”
* “My instincts say stay low…”
* “The real threat is near…”

Now each clue feels like:

> Spider-Man trying to see through the illusion.

Much stronger.

---

# FINALIZED RULE FLOW

## Mode Start

* 10 shots lit
* one hidden Super Illusion Jackpot
* jackpot = 1M

---

## Wrong standard shot

* 50K live score
* jackpot -100K

---

## Wrong clue shot

* 50K live score
* jackpot -25K
* Spidey Sense directional clue given

Examples:

* right / left
* upper / lower
* near / far
* bank / lane region

---

## Correct shot

* collect current jackpot
* Mysterio defeated

---

## Jackpot floor

* minimum 300K

---

# WHY THIS MODE NOW WORKS

Before:
random hidden award.

Now:

> narrowing field through intuition while value slips away.

Player feels:

* “I know more than I did”
* “but I’m losing money while searching”

That is exactly the tension we needed.

# EXTRA NICE TOUCH (RECOMMENDED)

* dim eliminated areas
  or

Not enough to reveal, just reinforce the spoken clue.

This helps players in noisy gameplay.
